Personal Brand

SOFTWARE: Photoshop, Indesign, Illustrator

TASK: Develop a personal brand including a stationary set and a giveaway element

CONCEPT: I knew I wanted this brand identity system to feel modern, simple, and visually interesting. I also really wanted to ensure the brand reflected both my technology background, as well as my design background. Therefore, each of my elements including my color scheme, pattern, stationary elements, and giveaway item each follow a very simple yet appealing design with a tagline that highlights this interest in tech.

PROCESS: Ideate, Sketch, Design

CHALLENGES: Being able to differentiate between a simple design, and a design that was boring.

TRIUMPHS: One portion of this project I am very proud of is the pattern I created.

LESSONS LEARNED: As for designing, this project also made me realize how important even the simplest elements, such as whitespace, can be in conveying meaning.
