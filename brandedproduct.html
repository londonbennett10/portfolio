Movie Poster

(not affiliated with the film)

SOFTWARE: Photoshop

TASK: Develop a movie poster for the film First We Bombed New Mexico

CONCEPT: For this poster, I knew I wanted to portray the grief and sadness this group of people had gone through. As a result I found assets that stuck out to me. I also really liked the inclusion of only black and white elements and a singular color of red. I felt red was the perfect color to depict the loss and pain.

CHALLENGES: Though I was very happy with how my poster looked, the clients were not. I had to add an element that the clients wanted which was a challenge and roadblock along the way.

TRIUMPHS:The final product.

LESSONS LEARNED: A lesson I was able to learn was in regards to how working with clients can be for a design. It showed how important it is to prioritize the desires of the client over your own.
