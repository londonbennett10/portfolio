Song of the Day

SOFTWARE: Photoshop

TASK: Create a collage using a variety of images influenced by a song of the day

CONCEPT: For this project, creating a collage for the song of the day was an idea that really appealed to me. As I began to develop these collages, they tendedto have a retro feel to them, even if the song was newer. Thus, I decided to have that as the overarching theme that would tie all the song collages together. I also included using the same typeface in each of them.

PROCESS: First step that came was choosing a song. Then came finding photos. Then came designing!

CHALLENGES: As I began this project the first major challenge I encountered was with the photos. I often struggled with finding photos that properly fit whatever vision I had in my head. .

TRIUMPHS: One triumph I had was working in photoshop and gaining familiarity.

SKILLS LEARNED: Photoshop, Photo manipultion, type handling
