Happy Place Postcard

SOFTWARE: Illustrator

TASK: Design a postcard that depicts your "happy place"

CONCEPT: I really had to sit and reflect to decide on a place that I would be able to accurately portray its importance and location. I ultimately landed on a coffee shop just down the street from my house known as Graham's 318. Grahams has been a place I could go to study or to get coffee or just get away from home. I always try to sit in this corner of the coffee house that features a striking blue couch and fun moon lamp above. The spot is cozy and without a doubt picturesque.

CHALLENGES: The illustration.

TRIUMPHS: I'm ultimately really happy with the illustration, even though I was definitely a bit out of my design comfort zone.

LESSONS LEARNED: This Projects showed the benefits of stepping out of my comfort zone. I was ultimately able to strengthen my Illustration skills.
